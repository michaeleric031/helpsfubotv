How to Sign In on a Smart TV or Connected Device
------------------------------------------------

On Smart TVs and media players (e.g., Roku, Apple TV, Fire TV, Android TV), the login process is slightly different.

1. Install the Fubo TV app from the device’s app store.  
2. Open the Fubo TV app.  
3. Select the **Sign In** option.  
4. Many devices will display an **activation code** on screen.  
5. On your phone or computer, visit **https://fubo.tv/connect**.  
6. Sign in with your Fubo TV email and password.  
7. Enter the activation code exactly as shown on your TV.  
8. Press **Submit** — your TV will automatically log in once validated. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
